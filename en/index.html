Applied Cases

Representative applications

Examples of shipbuilding and manufacturing scan data connected to inspection, recognition, and automation data.

2025

Deep-learning spatial recognition module for a container-ship cargo hold

Structures and workspaces are recognized from a shipyard cargo-hold point cloud, with object segmentation, spatial relationships, and inspection-reference data organized into an automation module.

  • InputCargo-hold 3D point cloud
  • AnalysisObject recognition, spatial reasoning, accessibility
  • OutputInspection and work-reference data package
2020-2021

3D point-cloud object detection in an LNGC cargo hold

Deep-learning point-cloud processing and post-processing were studied to detect and segment scaffolding systems during cargo-hold flatness measurement.

  • InputLNGC cargo-hold scan data
  • AnalysisObject detection, semantic segmentation, post-processing
  • OutputMeasurement-target organization and inspection support
2026

Inspection of Ship Block Support Installation Accuracy Using Object Detection and Spatial Data Post-processing Algorithms Based on 3D Point Cloud

Point clouds captured from 16 LiDAR scan stations beneath a ship block at an operating shipyard were semantically segmented, then processed to extract individual support centers and heights and quantify installation deviations against a reference layout.

  • Input3D LiDAR scans from 16 stations beneath a ship block
  • AnalysisPointVector semantic segmentation, DBSCAN clustering, RANSAC shape fitting
  • OutputIndividual support centers, heights, and quantified installation deviations
2025

Topology- and Geometry-Augmented Lightweight Shipyard Point-Cloud Datasets

Topologically significant structures and local geometric features were extracted from large-scale ship-block and shipyard point clouds to reduce data volume while preserving the shape information and performance required for deep-learning object detection.

  • InputLarge-scale ship-block and shipyard-environment 3D point clouds
  • AnalysisTDA persistent homology, FPFH geometric feature extraction and augmentation
  • OutputLightweight training datasets preserving shape quality and object-detection performance
2016-2021

3D scanning and shape-deviation analysis for small craft and FRP molds

Scan data from small craft and production molds were compared against reference shapes to verify dimensional control and process-improvement potential.

  • InputSmall-craft and FRP mold scan data
  • AnalysisRegistration, deviation map, quality criteria
  • OutputMold quality-control and improvement evidence
2025-2026

Point-cloud Physical AI and collaborative-robot workability study

Ship-block point-cloud environments, 3D vision sensors, and 3D-printed specimens are connected to assess robot paths, collision, accessibility, and weld-line extraction.

  • InputShip-block point cloud, robot cell, 3D vision conditions
  • AnalysisSpatial reasoning, collision, weld-line and surface extraction
  • OutputPhysical AI setup and robot execution data
